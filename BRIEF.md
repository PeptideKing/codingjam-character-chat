# Week 8: AI Character Chat (MMV)

## 1. The Contract (I/O)

**Input:** Character definition (name, 1-paragraph personality, 1 thing they'd never say) + chat text.

**Output:** A back-and-forth chat window (up to 5 messages).

## 2. The Magical Moment

Bringing an idea to life ("I made this person and I'm talking to them!").

## 3. Scope Discipline (What we are NOT building)

Short interactions only. No persistent memory, character avatars, voice generation, or multi-character worlds.

## 4. Personality / Signature Detail

The open canvas. Proving that by defining constraints (especially what a character won't say), you create a deeply authentic interaction.
